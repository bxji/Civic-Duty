API's available:

  • /representative/ -- gets representatives with POST, "zip": "yourzipcode"
  • /elections/ -- upcoming elections
  • /polling/ -- get polling places/mail in vote stuff and ballot info for candidates & measures -- requires following through POST
    • id -- election id, should be gotten through representative API
    • street -- your street address
    • city -- your city
    • zip -- your zip code
